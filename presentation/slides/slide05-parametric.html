Parametric Modeling

Why It Powers BIM Software Like Revit

What is Parametric Modeling?

Instead of drawing fixed geometry, you define rules and relationships between elements. Parameters and constraints drive the geometry. Change one thing and related elements update automatically.

Why It's Critical for BIM:

Automatic Coordination — Move a wall, doors, windows, and dimensions update instantly

Rapid Iteration — Explore design options without manual redrafting

Data-Driven Accuracy — Quantities, costs, schedules always correct

Example in Revit:

Change a wall's Height from 3000mm to 3500mm:

→ All windows on that wall adjust position

→ All dimensions update to show 3500mm

→ All schedules recalculate automatically

→ Room areas and volumes update in all views

Why This Matters:

Parametric modeling makes BIM intelligent — every element knows its relationship to others, enabling accurate coordination across the entire project.
