What is Autodesk Revit?

The Industry Standard BIM Software

Autodesk Revit is the world's leading BIM (Building Information Modeling) software used by architects, structural engineers, MEP engineers, and contractors to design, visualize, and deliver building projects.

Architecture

Design building layouts, walls, roofs, floors, doors, and windows

Create 3D visualizations and construction documents

Structure

Design foundations, beams, columns, and steel framing systems

Perform structural analysis and detailing

MEP Engineering

Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing systems design

HVAC ductwork, wiring, and pipe routing
